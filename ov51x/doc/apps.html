Test system is a PIII 600 running RedHat 7.3. Video card is an ATI All-in-Wonder 16MB (PCI), driven by XFree86 4.0.3 at 16 bpp.

NOTES:
  • Orange text indicates a feature not supported by the driver
  • * Driver only supports widths/heights that are a multiple of 16
  • ** Defaults to greyscale. For color, you must load ov511 with "force_palette=15".

Types are:

  • M: Motion video display
  • C: Motion video capture
  • T: Television capabilities
  • W: Webcam CGI
  • S: Single image capture
  • V: Videoconference
  • O: Other
